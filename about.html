Hello I'm

Iman Sara Haji

A Civil Engineering Student and Freelance Artist

I’m Iman, originally from the Seattle, Washington, my hometown! I'm currently a civil engineering student at the University of Washington entering my final year! Growing up, I've always had a passion for art, and quarantine has given me the time and inspiration to reconnect with art! I work with a wide range of mediums–watercolor, gouache, acrylic, ink, and colored pencil–creating elegant and vibrant artworks of nature, skies, cities, books, and anime!

As a self taught artist, I aspire to always evolve and improve my techniques to produce works that are meaningful and bring joy to others. I gather most of my inspiration from the nature around me in the beautiful Pacific Northwest, along with people and places I love! Through my wood paintings, bookmarks, greetings cards, and canvas paintings, I hope to inspire others to create and spark happiness in others.
