About Me

Hi, my name is Iman Haji!

Hi! I'm Iman, an undergraduate student pursuing my Bachelor's of Science in Civil Engineering at the University of Washington.

Originally from Mill Creek, Washington, I dropped out of high school to attend university at the age of 16. I have been awarded numerous scholarships and awards for my notable achievements and ambitious drive to excel in academics and industry.
